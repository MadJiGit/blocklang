Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about BlockLang browser extension

BlockLang is a privacy-focused browser extension that helps you clean up your search results by filtering unwanted content. It allows you to block specific domains, languages, or sites from appearing in your Google and Bing search results.

Yes, BlockLang is completely private and secure. All filtering happens locally on your device - no data is sent to external servers. Your browsing habits, search queries, and filter preferences never leave your device.

BlockLang is available for:

  • Chrome - Available on Chrome Web Store
  • Firefox - Available on Firefox Add-ons
  • Safari - Available on Mac App Store and iOS App Store

BlockLang offers both free and Pro versions:

  • Free Version - Up to 5 domain filters + Quick Russian Filter
  • Pro Version - Unlimited domain filters + all features

The free version is perfect for most users who want to block a few specific sites.

Setting up filters is simple:

  1. Click the BlockLang extension icon in your browser
  2. Add domains you want to block (e.g., facebook.com)
  3. Use wildcards for broader filtering (e.g., *.ru)
  4. Add language filters (e.g., language:ru)
  5. Toggle the Quick Russian Filter for instant blocking

Your filters will immediately start working on Google and Bing searches.
