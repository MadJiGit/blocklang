Why BlockLang?

The Story Behind It All.

From the team of BlockLang, we want to share the story of why we built this extension and how it can help you.

Why We Built This

As people who spend a lot of time online searching for information, We were constantly frustrated by the same recurring problems. When we searched for something in Bulgarian, Google often returned results in Russian – they sound similar enough, but the content was completely irrelevant and just wasted our time.

The other big issue was this constant "noise" from clickbait sites and fake news that cluttered up our search results, making it really frustrating to find quality, reliable information. We wanted something simple and discreet that would filter this out without getting in my way.

So we decided to build a browser extension to solve our own problems. We started with a Chrome version, just to see if the idea worked. Once we saw how much it helped us, we expanded it to Safari for macOS and iOS – for people like us who use Apple devices.

Whether you're doing research, learning something new, or just browsing – BlockLang lets you take control of your search results. Minimal, effective, and made with privacy in mind.

About

BlockLang is a simple browser extension designed to filter unwanted content and remove distracting or low-quality websites from your search results. Whether you're researching, working, or just browsing — BlockLang helps you stay focused by keeping irrelevant links out of sight.
