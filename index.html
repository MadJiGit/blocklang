About

BlockLang is a simple browser extension designed to filter unwanted content and remove distracting or low-quality websites from your search results. Whether you're researching, working, or just browsing — BlockLang helps you stay focused by keeping irrelevant links out of sight.

Search Result Filter & Privacy Tool

BlockLang helps you clean your search results by hiding distracting or unwanted websites — so you can focus on what matters.

  • Extension Name: BlockLang
  • Website: https://blocklang.org
  • Platform: Chrome, Firefox, Safari (iOS)
  • Developed by: MadJi
  • Version: 1.3.0
  • License: MIT
  • Contact: support@blocklang.org
  • Status: Active

Whether you're doing research, learning something new, or just browsing – BlockLang lets you take control of your search results. Minimal, effective, and made with privacy in mind.
