Block Websites by Language

BlockLang helps you remove unwanted foreign-language websites from your search results. Whether you're browsing Google, Bing, or other search engines, our extension filters out irrelevant content and keeps your experience focused and distraction-free.

Our language-based filtering system supports over 100 languages and provides real-time blocking, ensuring you only see content in the languages you understand or prefer.
